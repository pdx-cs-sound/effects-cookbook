Designing a compressor — five implementations as paths

You start with no design and choose one option at each decision; the series of choices is your path, and every path ends at a complete compressor. Each line is one open-source compressor from thirdparty/compare/. Toggle them to see that "a compressor" is a region of paths, not one algorithm.

Where two implementations make the same choice their lines run parallel (nudged apart so both stay visible). dafx and pcs detect on RMS; the rest on peak/abs. pcs is the outlier — no smoothing, no ballistics, auto makeup — i.e. a block-wise demo rather than a time-domain processor. Data adapted from thirdparty/compare/analysis.md.
