# Tremolo

*Chapter 5. Not yet written — see [Status & scope](status.md).*

Volume varied by an oscillator rather than by the signal itself: a parametric,
automatically controlled volume knob. The rest of this chapter —
[Compression](compression.md), [Limiting](limiter.md), [Expanding](expander.md),
[AGC](agc.md) — replaces the oscillator with a level detector, so the volume knob follows
the signal instead of a clock.
